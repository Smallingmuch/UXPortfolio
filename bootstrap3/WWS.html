Project Overview

Wish Watch Cinema is a cinema company that needed an app for their cinemas that will enable their users to book tickets online and a bonus point, by letting their users download old classics that they love.

The app was built for movie goers who don't have the luxry of time to buy physical tickets. It lets them book e-tickets for movies they wanted to watch.
The app gives lots of information about a movie with the trailer of the movie to help users decide if it is the right choice for them.

Role: UX Designer, UX Researcher and Visual Basic Designer

Tools:Figma, Adobe Xd

Release: Still in Progress
